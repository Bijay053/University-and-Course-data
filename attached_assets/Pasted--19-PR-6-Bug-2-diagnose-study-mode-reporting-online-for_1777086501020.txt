#19 - PR-6 Bug 2 — diagnose study_mode reporting "online" for ASA on-campus courses
What & Why
ASA's Bachelor of Business course page literally says <strong>Delivery</strong> Face to Face on campus in adjacent course-header-text divs. The current extractors/study_mode.py returns "online" instead, almost certainly because it picks up the word "Online" from the Location field (which reads "Sydney, Online"). This is a real production bug — every ASA course is being mis-classified. The user explicitly said "diagnose before patching"; do not jump to a fix until the actual extractor input → output mapping is captured on paper.
