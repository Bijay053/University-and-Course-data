
**Step 1.5 — ACU diagnostic (2 min)**

```
sudo -u postgres psql university_portal -c "
SELECT status, auto_publish_status, COUNT(*),
       MIN(created_at) AS oldest, MAX(created_at) AS newest,
       ROUND(AVG(completeness)) AS avg_completeness
FROM scraped_courses
WHERE university_id = 51
GROUP BY status, auto_publish_status
ORDER BY status, auto_publish_status;"
```

This tells us:
- How many ACU rows are staged and in what state
- Whether they're recently scraped (use as-is) or stale (rescrape first)
- Average completeness (will the rows pass the auto-publish gate?)

Don't promote ACU yet — just understand state. ACU promotion is for tomorrow if it's clean, or after rescrape if stale.
