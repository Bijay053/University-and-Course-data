**Mild pushback — what about regex-has-PTE-but-not-IELTS?**

The pre-skip checks `ielts_overall` from regex. What about courses where regex finds PTE or TOEFL but no IELTS? Vision would still fire on tier-1 images and could hallucinate an IELTS value into the empty slot.

Possibly an edge case at Flinders — their FIELD SUMMARY logs consistently showed IELTS via regex when *any* English data was found via regex. But for other unis, IELTS could be on a different page from PTE/TOEFL.

Proposal: the pre-skip check could be "any English test from regex" rather than just IELTS. If regex got *any* English data, tier-1 vision contributes only noise. If regex got nothing at all, that's the genuine ASAHE case.
