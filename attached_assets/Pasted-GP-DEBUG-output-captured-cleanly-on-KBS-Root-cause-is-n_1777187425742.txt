GP-DEBUG output captured cleanly on KBS. Root cause is now definitively identified, and there are two related bugs in the input preprocessing.

## The smoking gun (2 things)

**Bug 1: Hard 8005 char truncation on every single course**

From the GP-DEBUG output, every single course shows:
```
text_len=8005
text_len=8005
text_len=8005
text_len=8005
... (29 courses, all exactly 8005)
```

The ONE exception is `non-award-single-subject-enrolment` which has `text_len=6815` (because the source page is shorter). Every other course gets truncated at exactly 8005 characters.

**Bug 2: text[:500] is 100% nav/footer boilerplate, not course content**

Sample from KBS Master of Information Technology:
```
text[:500]="Master of Information Technology - Kaplan Business School Skip to main content 
Current Students Agent Resources Find An Agent News Alumni Contact Us Main navigation 
Courses Admissions Student Life Blog About Us Apply My saved courses (0) search 
Courses Master's Degrees MBA – Master of Business Administration Master of Business 
Analytics Master of Business Analytics (Extension) Master of Business Analytics 
(Information Technology) Master of Information Technology Master of Information 
Technology..."
```

Every course's first 500 chars is just the global nav menu listing every other course. The course title appears in position 0-50, then it's pure navigation/menu/footer text for the rest. The actual fee/IELTS/duration content presumably sits AFTER position 8005 — which gets truncated away.

## Combined effect

- `html_to_text` is walking DOM order naively (header → nav → main → footer)
- Output is truncated at exactly 8005 chars
- The 8005 chars hit before the main content area starts
- Gemini receives ~2780 tokens of pure navigation menu
- Gemini correctly returns nulls because the data isn't there

## Gemini's own reasoning confirms this

Look at these GP-DEBUG raw_response samples:
```
raw_response='" is mentioned. The prompt says "Default to \'On Campus\' when a physical location is mentioned."'
raw_response='Campus Locations" and "Online Study" are mentioned in the header/footer links.\n *'
raw_response='`fee`: null (not in text).\n * `domestic_fee`: null (not'
```

Gemini is literally telling us "these are mentioned in the header/footer links" and "not in text". The model is performing correctly — it just doesn't have the data because we never sent it the data.

## What to fix

**Two changes needed in the Gemini-primary input preprocessing:**

1. **Strip nav/header/footer BEFORE running `html_to_text`**
   - Use a content extraction approach that targets `<main>`, `<article>`, or the primary content div
   - For KBS specifically: course content sits inside specific Drupal content blocks (look at the rendered HTML structure)
   - At minimum: filter out elements with class names containing "nav", "header", "footer", "menu", "breadcrumb"

2. **Remove or dramatically raise the 8005 char truncation**
   - Why 8005? That's a suspiciously specific number — grep the code for `8000` or `8005`
   - With Gemini Flash Lite's context window (1M tokens), there's no need to truncate at 8K chars
   - Increase to at least 50,000 chars (still well within budget at ~12,500 tokens input)
   - OR remove the truncation entirely

## Validation criteria for the fix

After the fix, the GP-DEBUG output for KBS Master of Information Technology should show:
- `text_len` significantly larger than 8005 (10,000+ chars)
- `text[:500]` containing actual course content (fees, durations, IELTS scores) — NOT just nav menu
- `raw_response` containing populated values like `{"international_fee": 43680, "duration_value": 20, "duration_unit": "Month", "ielts_overall": 6.0, ...}`

Then the database should show:

```
           29 |        20+ |       <9
```
