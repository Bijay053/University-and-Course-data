**Why this matters more than the original Flinders concerns**

The whole reason we did three Flinders re-scrapes was to clean up English data on ~95 courses. After today's fixes the catalog has gone from 145 dirty rows to 107 clean rows, but the 38-row reduction is mostly real on-campus courses being silently dropped, not just the postgrad coursework hold-back we discussed. **Bachelor of Arts is a flagship course; missing it from a 12-uni catalog is more visible to end users than the PhD English fields we've been chasing.**

Needs urgent fix before promote.

**Suggested fix shape**

The stager's `online_only` filter needs to be "page explicitly says online students only" (which is what `domestic_only` does — it checks page text for "domestic students only"), not "study_mode field equals Online." A course can be available online and on-campus to international students; that's `Blended`, not `online_only`. Worth checking what predicate the stager is using and tightening it to require explicit page-text evidence rather than the structural `study_mode` value.

Separately, the `study_mode:rule` extractor for Flinders course pages needs a look — it's defaulting to `Online` when both modes are present. Lower priority; can be a phase-2 fix.
