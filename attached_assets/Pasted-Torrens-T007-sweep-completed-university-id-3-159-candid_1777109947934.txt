Detection has to be content-based. Visit one of the user's examples like `https://www.torrens.edu.au/courses/design/3d-design-animation` and look at the HTML structure. Strong signals for category landing:
- Page contains many outbound links to other `/courses/<faculty>/*` URLs (5+)

- H1/title doesn't start with degree-level word ("Bachelor", "Master", "Diploma", "Certificate", "Graduate", "Doctor", "Advanced Diploma", "Associate Degree")
- Has "explore", "discover", "find", "choose your" text in main content

Reject in `_is_category_landing` (or whatever the equivalent function is) BEFORE staging, not after. Don't stage them at 38-54% completeness with placeholder values — just skip.

**Lower priority bugs found (file separately, ship later, not this PR):**

**Bug D: Float precision in duration**
Multiple rows show `1.7000000476837158 Year`. That's a 32-bit float upcast to 64-bit. Round to 1 decimal in `duration.py` before serializing.

**Bug E: Course name normalization, vol 2**
VIT exposed prefix issues. Torrens exposes title-case issues:
- "Of" / "And" / "In" capitalized (should be lowercase prepositions): `Diploma Of 3d Design And Animation` → `Diploma of 3D Design and Animation`
- "3d" → "3D" (Roman/numeric+letter pattern)
- "Mba" → "MBA", "Vet" → "VET", "Ux" → "UX" (acronyms)
- Redundant suffixes like "Master of Business Administration Mba" (drop trailing acronym already in title)

This is the SAME `course_name.py` extractor that surfaced VIT issues. Worth one comprehensive PR fixing all the title-case rules in one go: prepositions lowercase, acronyms uppercase, alphanumeric mixes uppercase, dedupe acronym in title.

**Bug F: Course Location empty for all Torrens courses**
Every row shows `-` for Course Location. Torrens has Sydney/Melbourne/Adelaide/Brisbane campuses listed on their pages but extractor isn't catching them. Add Torrens-specific location extraction OR generalize T003 VIT static fallback to also fire on `torrens.edu.au`.

**Bug G: AI fallback hallucinating durations**
Category landing pages are getting impossible durations from AI fallback:
- `Bachelor Of Health Science Naturopathy: 8 Year` (real value: 4 years)
- `Bachelor Of Interior Design Residential: 6 Year` (real value: 3 years)
- `Faculty Of Health: 10 Year` (it's a faculty, not a course)
- `Health Science: 10 Year` (category)

If Bug A lands first, this mostly self-resolves (these rows won't be staged). But duration AI fallback should also have a sanity gate: reject duration values > 5 years for non-Doctorate level.

**Bug H: Browser timeouts on torrens.edu.au, same pattern as VIT vocational and CSU**
~30+ timeouts at 20s on torrens. Each costs 30s wall time. Static fallback catches them but doubles the runtime. Same broader fix needed as VIT vocational — per-host config or path-specific browser strategy.
