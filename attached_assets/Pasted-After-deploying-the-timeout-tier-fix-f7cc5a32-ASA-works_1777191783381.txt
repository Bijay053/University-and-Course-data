After deploying the timeout tier fix (`f7cc5a32`), ASA works perfectly. But KBS now exhibits the same browser-timeout pattern that ASA had:

```
[FALLBACK] [per-course browser ↻] https://www.kbs.edu.au/courses/master-of-business-analytics
[FALLBACK] timeout: per-course browser exceeded 20s on https://www.kbs.edu.au/courses/master-of-business-analytics — moving on
[GP-DEBUG] static=140609B rendered=0B using=static
```

Nearly every KBS course page (~25 of 29) times out at 20s. KBS is currently in the default tier (20s/domcontentloaded), but it needs the same `_SLOW_HOSTS` treatment as ASA.

## Why KBS data still looks correct

KBS gets away with browser timeouts because:
1. KBS publishes English requirements on a central page (cached at university level)
2. KBS publishes fees in a central PDF (cached at university level)
3. The central caches are populated from earlier successful scrapes

So even with `rendered=0B` everywhere, the central page fallback fills in the right values via:
```
[CENTRAL ✓] Master of Business Analytics — fee from 'Master of Business Analytics' row: intl=40800.0
[CENTRAL ✓] Master of Business Analytics — english from central page: pte=50.0 ielts=6.0 toefl=72.0 cambridge=169.0
```

This works as long as central caches don't invalidate. If they do (cache TTL expires, or someone deletes them), KBS will start showing empty English/fees just like ASA did.

## Fix

Add `kbs.edu.au` to `_SLOW_HOSTS` in `per_course_browser.py`. Same 60s outer / 50s goto / networkidle that ASA gets.

The tier should now be:

| Host | Strategy | Outer | Goto |
|------|----------|-------|------|
| `asahe.edu.au` | networkidle | 60s | 50s |
| `kbs.edu.au` | networkidle | 60s | 50s |
| `vit.edu.au` / `csu.edu.au` | networkidle | 30s | 25s |
| Default | domcontentloaded | 20s | 15s |

## Optional: Reconsider the 20s default

The 20s/`domcontentloaded` default seems too aggressive. KBS, ASA — both real Australian education sites — don't fit in 20s. May be worth raising the global default to 30s in the same PR. But this is optional; the immediate fix is just adding KBS.
